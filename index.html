ABOUT ME

At the begging of 2020, I decided to pursue a new and exciting career in software development. To fulfill this objective I attended Le Wagon Bootcamp (full-time) in Lisbon which I have graduated in July 2020 (batch #419). Looking forward to my next challenge where I can become a great developer, work on meaningful products, and be an active member of the tech community.
